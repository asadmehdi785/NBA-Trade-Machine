Privacy

We at Revenge of the Dreamers are committed to the absolute privacy of our users. As we never require you to input an email address associated with your account, you can be sure that you will not receive any annoying emails or spam from us. In addition, we make sure all passwords are hashed, making it much more difficult for your account to be compromised. We do not share any information associated with your account to any third-parties, and we strongly recommend against sharing your passwords with anyone in order to secure your account.
